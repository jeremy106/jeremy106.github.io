Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My plan is to find a job as a junior/intermediate developer and gain experience in a reasonably mature tech/SaaS company. Eventually I hope to come up with some ideas / products to develop personally both as a hobby and as a secondary source of income.

What are your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning?

I am confident that I can learn and understand anything if I'm given the time to think it through. I struggle if information is dumped on me in no particular order or without structure and I don't like learning things before they are relevant to me. If I'm asked to do something myself and given some kind of starting point, I usually figure things out on my own pretty quickly.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to get better at sharing my work/processes with other people and to seek feedback earlier and more often. I have a tendancy to isolate myself until I think everything is perfect before sharing my work with others.

How will you manage your workload in this programme?

I expect to have no issues managing my workload for this programme. I have no dependents and a supportive partner who is ready to leave me to my own devices as required over the coming weeks/months. I intend to strictly enforce some periods of 'downtime' in the late evenings and weekends to prevent myself from burning out.

If things become stressful, I always know I can straighten things out by doing some exercise. I have years of elite sports experience to fall back on here.

If I find myself stuck on something for more than an hour I intend to seek help from my Facilitator or a peer who I consider knowledgeable.

What do you expect from the Facilitation team?

The main thing I'm expecting from the Facilitation team is for feedback and encouragement to push myself. I aim for excellence in all things so I will not be upset to have mistakes pointed out or to be given suggestions on how I can make something even better.

Is there any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things?

I hope to maintain a weekly hobby night that I host with some friends on Thursday evenings however I will be willing to give this up during bootcamp if the workload makes this untenable.
